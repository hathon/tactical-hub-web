About Us

We build and scale tactical & outdoor brands in Central Europe
Tactical Hub is a fast-growing commerce and distribution company focused on the tactical, outdoor, and travel gear market.
We operate a portfolio of specialized e-commerce stores and media-driven brands, each targeting a distinct customer segment within the outdoor and tactical ecosystem.

Our mission is simple:
to help high-quality brands enter, grow, and dominate the Central & Eastern European market.

We are not just a reseller.
We act as a strategic partner for brands, combining:
e-commerce infrastructure
performance marketing

local market expertise

This allows us to launch and scale products efficiently in a highly engaged and fast-growing niche.

We operate and scale e-commerce businesses across the tactical, outdoor, and travel segments.

Central and Eastern Europe is one of the fastest-growing e-commerce regions in the EU, driven by increasing online adoption and a rapidly expanding customer base.
This creates strong opportunities for brands to grow quickly with the right local partner.

Psy Goncze

Psy Gończe is a specialized e-commerce store focused on tactical gear, shooting accessories, and everyday carry equipment.
It targets a highly engaged audience of professionals, enthusiasts, and outdoor users who value reliability, performance, and proven products.

Nomadzi

Nomadzi is an outdoor and travel-focused e-commerce platform designed for modern explorers. It offers carefully selected gear for hiking, camping, and long-distance travel, targeting customers who value independence, mobility, and real-world usability.

Nomari

Nomari is a content-driven brand focused on modern travel, gear, and exploration. It combines storytelling, product discovery, and curated recommendations to build trust and engagement with a new generation of travel-oriented customers.
